Privacy Policy — ChatFlow Elite

Last updated: March 2026
Summary: ChatFlow Elite does NOT collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All your data stays on your device in Chrome's local storage.

1. What Data We Collect

ChatFlow Elite stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Canned responses you create (titles, text, categories)
  • Extension settings (theme, language, feature toggles)
  • Activity metrics (reply counts, timestamps — for your personal dashboard only)
  • Employee name (optional, entered by you)
  • API keys you provide (Gemini AI, Telegram bot — stored in Chrome's local storage)

2. What Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT read, store, or transmit the contents of your ManyChat, Telegram, or WhatsApp conversations
  • We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • We do NOT send any data to our servers — we have no servers
  • We do NOT use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party SDKs
  • We do NOT access your contacts, browsing history, or other extensions

3. Third-Party Services

If you choose to enable optional integrations, data flows as follows:

  • Google Gemini AI: When you use AI features, your message text is sent to Google's Gemini API using your own API key. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sheets (optional): Activity data is sent to your own Google Apps Script webhook. You control this data entirely.
  • Telegram Bot (optional): Alert messages are sent via your own Telegram bot token. Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API, which means:

  • Data is stored only on your local device
  • Data is removed when you uninstall the extension
  • You can clear all data at any time via Chrome's extension settings

5. Permissions Justification

  • storage: Save your canned responses and settings locally
  • tabs: Detect which site you're on to enable the right features
  • scripting: Inject the canned responses drawer into supported sites
  • notifications: Show SLA alerts when response time exceeds your threshold
  • Host permissions (manychat.com, web.telegram.org, web.whatsapp.com): Required to inject the extension's UI into these specific sites

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
